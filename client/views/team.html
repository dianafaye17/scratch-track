About ScratchTrack

Manage your songwriting workflow on the go


ScratchTrack is an application designed both for easily capturing song ideas on the go and for managing on-going, multifaceted projects as they develop. Our interface allows a user create audio recordings at the click of a button, to write and revise lyrics, and to transcribe attractive guitar tablature. With ScratchTrack you can generate ideas, revisit past songs, or jump to the public page to explore our other user's projects.


Never let a song idea idea slip through your fingers again.
